Questions

The short answers.

Do I need a Jellyfin server?

Yes. Jellyfuse is a client — you bring your own server. We never see your data, your library, or your viewing habits.

Do I need Jellyseerr?

No. Requests are optional. If you don't run Jellyseerr, the request UI hides itself and Jellyfuse behaves as a pure Jellyfin client.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone today. iPad, Apple TV, Mac, and Android are in active development — see the Platforms section above for status on each.

Is it really free?

Yes. GPL-3.0 licensed, no paywall, no telemetry. Build it yourself from source, or grab the latest release through the App Store.

What about HDR and Dolby Vision?

HDR10 and Dolby Vision over HDR10 ship in an upcoming update. HLG works today on supported devices.

Why MPV?

One battle-tested playback engine on every platform means identical subtitle rendering, identical audio routing, and identical codec support — no per-device surprises.
