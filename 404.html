Yours, on your terms

Yours, end to end.

Jellyfuse talks only to your server. No analytics. No telemetry. No third-party SDKs. Inspect every byte if you want — the source is public.

Free, forever.

GPL-3.0 licensed. No Pro tier. No paywalled features. No accounts. Build it yourself or grab the latest release — the choice is yours.
Built right

The details that matter.

Nine years of Jellyfin clients have taught us what people actually use. We built those things first.

Offline downloads

Pause, resume, skip, retry. Built for spotty Wi-Fi and long flights.

Background audio

Lock the screen, keep listening. Audiobooks and concerts work the way you'd expect.

Picture in Picture

Float your show in a corner while you reply to that message. System-native PiP.

Trickplay scrubbing

Frame-accurate previews under the seek bar — no more guessing where you are.

Chapter markers

Jump straight to the cold open, the title sequence, or the credits. Honor your time.

Intro & outro skip

One tap. Already detected. Get to the story faster.

External subtitles

Drop in .srt or .ass — full styling, including karaoke and overrides. MPV does the rendering.

Multi-user profiles

Switch accounts in two taps. Each profile keeps its own resume points and settings.

Blended search

One search bar. Library hits and request candidates side by side, clearly labeled.
